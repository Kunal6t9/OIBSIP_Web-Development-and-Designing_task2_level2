Elon Musk

Innovator, Entrepreneur, Visionary

About Elon Musk

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He is a renowned entrepreneur and engineer who moved to the U.S. and studied at the University of Pennsylvania. He founded and contributed to several groundbreaking ventures like PayPal, Tesla, and SpaceX.

Major Achievements

  • SpaceX: Developed reusable rockets, first private company to launch astronauts to space.
  • Tesla: Pioneered the electric car revolution.
  • Neuralink: Working on brain-machine interfaces.
  • The Boring Company: Building futuristic transportation tunnels.
  • X (formerly Twitter): Acquired and restructured the social media platform.
